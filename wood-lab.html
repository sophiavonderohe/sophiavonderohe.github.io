Wood Lab (UW SAFS)

Parasite Ecology & Invasive Species

At the Wood Lab within the University of Washington School of Aquatic and Fishery Sciences, my work focuses on the intersection of disease ecology and marine conservation. Currently, I am involved in research regarding parasite-host interactions and the development of educational tools for marine biology.

RESEARCH

Invasive Species & Parasitism

Investigating how invasive species alter local parasite-host dynamics in Puget Sound.
EDUCATION

Teaching Module Development

Designing interactive modules to teach undergraduate students about aquatic disease ecology.
QUANTITATIVE

Spatial Data Analysis

Using R to map the distribution of specific marine parasites across varying environmental gradients.
